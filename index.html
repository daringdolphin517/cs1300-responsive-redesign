The North Dakota Rodeo Association:
A Needed Redesign


The Original Website: https://www.ndrodeo.com


The Issues At Hand

The original website featured many issues, regarding its usability, learnability, memorability, and accessibility.

Usability Issues:

  1. Website layout is counterintuitive, and picutres seem randomly arranged on the screen
  2. Website doesn't scale very well, phone and tablet sizes cause images to bleed and text to become somewhat unreadable.
  3. The headline rotation, while a nice idea, rotates too quickly and doesn't allow for digestable information. The majority of the space goes unused as well.
  4. The "Upcoming Events" section looks like there should be a list of events, but there is nothing there and no message explaining why it might be empty.
  5. The massive black border on either side is a waste of space and makes the text smaller.

Accessibility Issues:

  1. Alternative text for images is either non-existent or poorly written.
  2. Text and photo captions are too small
  3. Users are supposed to see two clear columns, but images and text on the left bleed over into an empty column on the right.
  4. Some links point to nothing or redirect - sometimes unclear these are links at all.
